About Us

Company Background

UT Generation Sdn. Bhd. (UTGSB) was formerly known as Iringan Khidmat Sdn. Bhd. and was incorporated on 25th October 1995. Its current official operational address is at No. 42A, Jalan Badminton 13/29, Section 13, 40100 Shah Alam, Selangor Darul Ehsan.

The Company's core business activities are mainly in the areas of Civil & Structural and Mechanical & Electrical Engineering works. The Company is managed by a team of result-oriented, energetic and visionary professionals entrusted to carry out tasks in the Construction Industry in particular, Design & Implementation of Overhead Transmission Systems, Domestic & Industrial Wirings and Infrastructure Works.

With vast experiences of over 28 years in Consulting and Construction sectors, the founders of UTGSB, Ir. Zainudin Mohd Salleh and Sulzaily A. Hamid had today embarked more aggressively into realizing the Company's goal to be a Leader in the Construction Industry by delivering the Highest level of Service Excellence through Consistency in Quality, Professionalism, Honesty, Trust and Integrity.

UTGSB is a 100% Bumiputra-owned Company. The Company has registered with PKK as Class 'A' & Class 'I' Contractor with Bumiputra status, CIDB under Grade '7', TNB as Class 'A' and Class 'I' Contractor, Suruhanjaya Tenaga as Class 'A' Contractor, Ministry of Finance with Bumiputra status, FELDA Holdings Berhad and various private sectors. UTGSB is professionally-positioned, well recognized and accepted in the business circles and enjoys good reputation and accreditation from clients and consultants alike.
